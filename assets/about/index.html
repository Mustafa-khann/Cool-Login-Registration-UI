Why and How StuCus Started?

So I was in my first year of computer science, highly motivated to work in Cyber-security and learn with people, who also like to work in Cyber-security. But i ended up finding only 4 guys in Peshawar, who liked to work in Cyber-security in (One Year). there were a lot of cyber-sec nerds out there but there was no Online platform for them. which led me to the thought, that there will be lots of other students like me who wants to work on different interesting projects and develop skill-sets and a supportive Tech and Business community. So I was like: Lets make such platform for students to work and build projects having mutual interests in Tech and Business.
